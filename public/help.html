pick phase: choose which heroes to play

click on a hero card in the index to bring it into your deck.

you can view hero details by hovering over any card with your mouse.

while the pick phase is ongoing, you can click on any card in your deck to remove it.

if you haven't picked any heroes by the time that the pick phase completes, you and your opponent will be returned to the lobby.

your turn: select a hero token and move it

click one of your tokens on the map to move it, and click again to place it.

the maximum distance that you can move the token depends on the hero's movement (MVT) stat.

in timed mythomagic, you have a limited amount of time in which to complete your turn.

choose an action: autoattack, use your ability, or use your ultimate

the hero's autoattack will attack all opponents within their reach (RC).

each hero has a unique ability and an ultimate. both of these typically consume mana points (MP).

all heroes gain MP after each round. autoattack grant extra MP to the character.

in addition to an ability and an ultimate, each hero has a passive that is automatically applied each turn.

damaging opponents

the base amount of damage done is calculated by the hero's ATK divided by their opponent's DEF.

there are six status effects that modify the base amount of damage:

  1. blinded: attacks performed by a blinded hero have a 75% chance of missing and dealing no damage. attacks targeting this hero deal 25% extra damage.
  2. charmed: the hero loses their defense stat, causing the base amount of damage to scale solely with ATK.
  3. distracted: the hero will not gain MP after a turn or after an autoattack.
  4. poisoned: the hero will lose a certain amount of HP (10% at moment of poisoning) each turn.
  5. stunned: the hero's ATK and MVT are reduced to zero, and RC is reduced to one.
  6. terrified: the hero's ATK is reduced by 50%.
  7. invisible: the hero's location is not visible to the opponent.

status effects expire after a certain number of turns and can also be removed by certain abilities.

some heroes have immunities to certain status effects.

example: a terrified hero with ATK=2000 would deal (2000/10)/2=100 HP of damage to a hero with DEF=10.

true damage is never affected by hero stats or statuses.

heroes are defeated when their HP reaches zero. defeated heroes can no longer be played in game, and their passives become inactive.

mythomagic is won when all enemy heroes are defeated, or your opponent concedes.
