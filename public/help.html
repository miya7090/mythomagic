how to play

(1/4) join a lobby

enter a nickname and choose one of four possible lobbies to play in: corinth (60-), athens (45-30), olympia (30-20), or sparta (10-10).

lobbies restrict the amount of time you have to pick a hero and to play your turn.

games of mythomagic played in corinth have a pick phase of 60 seconds and untimed turns.

athens is recommended for most games and gives 45 seconds to pick a hero and 25 seconds on each of your turns.

(2/4) choose which heroes to play

pick up to five hero cards by clicking in the index to bring a card into your deck.

you can view hero details by hovering over any card with your mouse, and you can click on any card in your deck to remove it.

if you haven't picked any heroes by the time that the pick phase completes, you and your opponent will be returned to the lobby.

(3/4) select a hero token and move it

each player has two turns per round.

on your turn, click one of your tokens on the map to move it, and click again to place it.

the maximum distance that you can move the token depends on the hero's movement (MVT) stat.

at any point, you can press z and x to decrease/increase the preview radius, which appears when you hover your cursor over a game tile.

the preview radius can be useful as a tool for measuring tile distances on the grid.

(4/4) autoattack, use your ability, or use your ultimate

the hero's autoattack will attack all opponents within their reach (RC).

each hero also has a unique ability consuming 200 mana points (MP), and an ultimate consuming 1000 MP.

all heroes gain a certain amount of MP after each round, and generally can accumulate up to 1000 MP.

in addition to an ability and an ultimate, each hero has a passive skill that is automatically applied each turn.

ability, ultimate, and passive descriptions can be previewed here at any time.

damaging opponents

the base amount of damage done is usually calculated with the hero's attack (ATK) divided by their opponent's defense (DEF).

there are six status effects that modify the base amount of damage:

  1. charmed: the hero's DEF is reduced to 1.
  2. distracted: the hero is prevented from gaining MP, and DEF is reduced by 25%.
  3. poisoned: the hero will obtain 500/DEF damage each turn.
  4. stunned: the hero's ATK and MVT are reduced to zero, and RC is reduced to one.
  5. terrified: the hero's ATK is reduced by 50%.
  6. obscured: the hero's location is not visible to the opponent.

as an example, a terrified hero with ATK=2000 would deal (2000/10)/2=100 HP of damage to a hero with DEF=10.

status effects typically persist for 4 turns; repeated applications will stack by duration. status effects can be removed by certain heroes.

true damage is applied directly and is not affected by hero stats or statuses.

heroes are defeated when their HP reaches zero. a hero's passive becomes inactive once they are defeated.

mythomagic is won when all enemy heroes have been defeated, or when your opponent concedes.
